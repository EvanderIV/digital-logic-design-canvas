Lab Information

Objective:

The goal of this laboratory exercise is to familiarize the student with the powerful commercial Altera Computer Aided Design tools and rapid prototyping technology. These goals will be taught in the context of interfacing the DE1 Programmable logic board to a Standard PS/2 Keyboard. This lab also serves to prepare the students for their own creative design which they will complete in Lab 8.

Key Words/Abbreviations:

FPGA (Field-Programmable Gate Array), Quartus II, PS/2 (Personal System/2) Keyboard
