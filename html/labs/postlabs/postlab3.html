Lab Information

Objective:

The purpose of this Laboratory will be to familiarize the student with the CEDARLogic, a Digital Logic Simulator. The student will do so in the context of developing good design technique and practice with NAND - NAND and NOR - NOR logic implementations.

Key Words/Abbreviations:

oscilloscope (scope), waveform, SOP, POS, timing diagram

Complete all the required elements for your report: Cover page, Procedure and Results, Conclusions, and Questions (below) specified in the Lab Introduction handout.
