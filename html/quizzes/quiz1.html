Quiz #1: Binary to Hexadecimal

EGCP 1010: Digital Logic Design

Quiz Information

This quiz will test your ability to quickly convert binary numbers to hexadecimal. There are 25 4-bit binary numbers, and you will have 60 seconds to complete the quiz.

Please memorize these conversions. You will do poorly if you attempt to convert them manually during the quiz. It is recommended to use flashcards as practice.
