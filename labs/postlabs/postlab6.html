Lab Information

Objective:

The purpose of this laboratory is to expose the student to a significant design problem, beginning with a word statement through implementation in the CedarLogic simulator.

Key Words/Abbreviations:

pop, tri-state buffer, bit slice, algorithm

Logistics:

This project will span two weeks, and will require a more substantial formal design report reflective of the complexity of this design.

Post Lab:

Follow closely the Lab 6 grade guide for this 150 point lab!

New: You are asked to write an abstract for this lab report. An abstract is a concise paragraph describing what is to follow in your report. It should give the reader enough detail to get the big picture of your design project. Place it right before the Procedures and Results.
