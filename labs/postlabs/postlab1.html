Lab Information

Objective:

The goal of this laboratory is to familiarize the student with the equipment, techniques and procedures used in the Digital Logic laboratory environment. The student should gain familiarity with the low-power Schottky (LS) transistor-transistor logic (TTL) chip family, the Complete Analog/Digital Electronics Trainer (CADET II), the FLUKE 45 digital multimeter (DMM), and tools such as chip pullers and wire strippers.

Key Words/Abbreviations:

bus, proto-board, fault, floating input, TTL, DMM, GND, VCC
